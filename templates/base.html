NECBDC

The North East Cane Bamboo Development Council (NECBDC) is a statutory body under the North Eastern Council (NEC), Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER), Government of India. The NECBDC was established in 1986 under the North Eastern Council Act, 1971. The NECBDC is a nodal agency for the development of cane and bamboo in the North Eastern Region (NER) of India.
